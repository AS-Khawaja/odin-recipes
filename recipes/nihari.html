Nihari

Description

Nihari is a deliciously smooth flour based stew with slow cooked mutton and a myriad of spices. This mutton nihari recipe takes ~4 hours to cook but its easy to put together. To get the best flavour, the mutton is cooked on very low heat for upto 4 hours

Ingredients

  • 1 kg mutton , preferably shank portion (cut into 8-10 pieces)
  • 4 tbsp Ghee/ Clarified butter
  • 2 medium onions , finely sliced
  • 1 tsp ginger paste

Steps

  1. 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  2. 2 tsp fennel seeds
  3. 1 tsp soonth dry ginger
  4. 5-6 green cardamoms
