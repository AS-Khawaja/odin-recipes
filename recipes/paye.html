Paye

Description

Paya is a traditional food from South Asia. It is served at various festivals and gatherings, or made for special guests. Paya means 'leg'/'feet' in Hindi and Urdu languages.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg mutton , preferably shank portion (cut into 8-10 pieces)
  • 4 tbsp Ghee/ Clarified butter
  • 2 medium onions , finely sliced
  • 1 tsp ginger paste

Steps

  1. 1 tbsp cumin seeds
  2. 2 tsp fennel seeds
  3. 1 tsp soonth dry ginger
  4. 5-6 green cardamoms
